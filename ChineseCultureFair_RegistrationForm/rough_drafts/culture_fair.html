Highlights and Key Topics

Paper-Cutting: Learn to make basic shapes Calligraphy: Learn basic strokes and how to write your first character Tai Chi: Start your morning with the relaxing and meditative practice that can transform your life with new peace of mind Tea Tasting: Learn to brew and sample a variety of Chinese teas Guzheng/Erhu: Get some practice playing traditional Chinese stringed instruments Qipao Fashion Show: Get to see some of the designs that have characterized mens' and womens' fashion in different time periods in China Cross-Talk: Try to keep up with the sharp wit of some mainland China's newest cross-talk comedians

Events

Paper-Cutting Workshop

In this 2-hour workshop, we get a brief but intensive intro to traditional Chinese paper-cutting. You'll learn how to cut out basic shapes and take the first step towards cutting more elaborate designs.

Calligraphy Workshop

In this 2-hour workshop, we get a brief but intensive intro to traditional Chinese calligraphy. You'll learn how to make simple strokes as well as draw your first character!

Tea Sampling

In this 1-hour session, a local tea shop owner demonstrates how to brew some well-known brands of Chinese tea, include Oolong tea as well as Pu'er tea(from Yunnan Province).

Guzheng for Beginners

In this 1-hour session, you'll learn to play some simple notes and hear the melody of this traditional Chinese instrument!

Cross-Talk Fun

In this 1-hour session, you'll get to listen to a mixed Chinese-English cross-talk dialogue by a young, post-80s cross-talk duo who are a hit sensation on the cross-talk circuit in mainland China.

Qipao Fashion Show

In this 1-hour session, our CHC girls display some traditional qiapos imported from specialty shops in mainland China as well as hand-sewn outfits passed down from elderly family members.
